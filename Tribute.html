Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The Misille Man

Here is the timeline of Dr. APJ badul Kalam
  • 1931. Born on 15 October 1931 to a Tamil Muslim family in Rameswaram
  • 1954. Graduated in physics in 1954 from Madras University
  • 1955. Moved to Madras in 1955 to study aerospace engineering in Madras Institute of Technology
  • 1980. Successfully deployed the Rohini satellite in near-earth orbit
  • 1969. Was transfered to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) as a project director
  • 1960. Joined Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)
  • 1970-1999. Achivements (Project Devil, Project Valiant, Agni, Prithvi, Chief Scientific Advisor to the Defence Research and Development Organisation)
  • 2002-2007. Served as Persident of India
  • 2015. He left the world
